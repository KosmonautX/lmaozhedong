PRIORITY

52. Charge Tsarapkin with informing Lancaster about Malik’s agreement to meet with an assistant of Acheson or one of the American ambassadors as Lancaster proposes.

Malik should listen to the State Department representative and in the event it is evident that the Americans are making a step forward in the direction of a peaceful settlement of the Korean question declare to him that he, Malik will thinking about the questions raised in the conversation and will give a reply in the next conversation.

Telegraph about the results.

At the instructions of Headquarters
