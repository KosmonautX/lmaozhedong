MID USSR

No. 13645

Tenth Department Received 4 hours 30 minutes 25/VII.1950

Dispatched 5 hours 55 minutes 25/ VII.1950

CIPHERED TELEGRAM

To Beijing To Soviet Ambassador Roshchin

SPECIAL

TOP PRIORITY

To Your No. 1503.

On the authorization of Filippov [Stalin], transmit to Mao Zedong or Zhou Enlai that we agree with the proposed procedure and time period for training Chinese pilots on jet planes.

Telegraph the fulfillment.

VYSHINSKY

25.VII.50

Copies: Stalin, Molotov, Malenkov, Vyshinsky, 10th Department, Copy.
