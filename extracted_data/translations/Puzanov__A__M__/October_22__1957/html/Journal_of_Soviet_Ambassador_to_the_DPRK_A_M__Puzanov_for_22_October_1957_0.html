…22 October 1957

Nam Il reported that the leadership welcomes the decision about a display of a Soviet mobile scientific and technical exhibit on the use of atomic energy for peaceful purposes and agrees with the time for the display of the exhibit in Pyongyang of June 1958.

Nam Il asked for assistance in obtaining permission to attach a baggage car to the Pyongyang-Moscow train which is to leave on 23 October. They intend to ship in this car the paperwork [oformlenie] for the DPRK workers delegation which will take part in the demonstration of workers on 7 November and also gifts which the Party-government delegation will take with them to Moscow. A telegram was sent to Moscow by the DPRK Ministry of Railways but there is as yet no answer.

I promised Nam Il to take the necessary measures.
