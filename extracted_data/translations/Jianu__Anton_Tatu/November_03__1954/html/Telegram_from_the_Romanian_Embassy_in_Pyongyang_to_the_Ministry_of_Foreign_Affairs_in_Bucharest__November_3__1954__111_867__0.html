Telegram from the Romanian Embassy in Pyongyang to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Bucharest, November 3, 1954, 111.867

On November 1, 1954, the Plenum of the Central Committee of the Workers’ Party of Korea took place, whose agenda included:

  1. Increasing productivity in agriculture;
  2. Electing the party leadership.

Comrade Kim Il, member of the Politburo, Vice-President of the Council of Ministers, and Minister of Agriculture, talked about the first point on the agenda

The plenum continues. No documents have been published so far.

Signed

Ambassador Tatu Jianu
