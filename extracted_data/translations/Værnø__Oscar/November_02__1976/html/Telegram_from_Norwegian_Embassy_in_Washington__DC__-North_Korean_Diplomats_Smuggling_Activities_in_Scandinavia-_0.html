ROYAL NORWEGIAN EMBASSY

WASHINGTON D.C.

2. NOVEMBER 1976

NO. 855

ATTACHMENT

ROYAL MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

NORTH KOREAN DIPLOMATS SMUGGLING ACTIVITIES IN SCANDINAVIA

The Sunday issue (31.10) of the Washington Post featured a longer, three-column, front page article on the North Korean diplomats’ smuggling activities in Scandinavia. The article, written by the newspaper’s London correspondent Bernard D. Nossiter and dated Oslo, 30. October, is attached to this dispatch.

To the Ambassador:

Oscar Værnø
